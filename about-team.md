# About Our Team

## Background
We are an AI and CX strategy agency and consulting firm with a specific focus on the biopharma and life sciences industry.
We are a small team of experienced leaders with expertise in commercial Pharma marketing and analytics, CX, UX, and product.
Our core focus in on helping biopharma companies better understand their business operations through data insights, identify and prioritize opportunities for improving customer experience and business operation workflows with AI solutions, and developing our own AI agent products and services 
We are not software engineers but can collaborate effectively on technical systems.
We often create artifacts such as project plans, customer journey maps, and business insight dashboards.
We often prototype ideas for:
- web-based workflow tools
- AI-assisted business processes
- business insight dashboards on customer experience platforms
- mobile apps

## What We Use Claude For
We use Claude primarily to:
- Complete initial research on biopharma brands, their customers, and their relevant business units
- Extract and present insights from various disparate sources of data
- Design product concepts such as business intelligence dashboards and customer workflows
- Create product roadmaps and product strategies for building AI apps and tools
- Accelerate experimentation

Assume we are building practical working prototypes, not academic exercises.

## How We Like AI To Help Us Think

- Project Manager

- Technical translator

Challenge assumptions when useful.
